# Digital Compass (Brulula Digital)
Helping older adults navigate the digital world safety

# Why I Started this proyect 
This project started with my grandmother.

While watching her use technology, I noticed how convincing AI-generated videos had become. Seeing how easily they could be mistaken for real content made me realize how vulnerable many older adults are to misinformation, deepfakes, and online scams.

At the same time, I noticed something else: technology was not always improving people's lives. I observed how excessive screen time and unbalanced technology use could negatively affect daily life, relationships, and overall well-being.

These experiences inspired me to create Digital Compass, an educational platform designed to help older adults navigate the digital world safely and confidently while also encouraging healthier digital habits.

Rather than focusing only on technical skills, the platform promotes digital literacy, critical thinking, and responsible technology use. Its goal is to empower older adults to recognize online risks, make informed decisions, and enjoy the benefits of technology without feeling overwhelmed or unsafe.

I believe technology should be a tool that improves people's lives—not something that confuses, manipulates, or controls them. Digital Compass was created with that vision in mind.

## Language
Although this README is written in English, most of the educational content is currently available in Spanish, as the project was originally created for older adults in Colombia, where Spanish is the primary language.

In the future, I plan to add a complete Spanish–English translation. This will allow the platform to reach a broader audience while continuing to support its original Spanish-speaking users.

## Project Status
This project is currently under active development. New educational modules, interactive activities, and an English translation are planned for future updates.
