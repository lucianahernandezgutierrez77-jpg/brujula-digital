# Digital Compass (Brulula Digital)
Helping older adults navigate the digital world safety

# Why I Started this proyect 
This proyect started with my Grandmother.

While watching her use technology, I noticed how convincing AI-generated videos had become. Seeing how easily they could be mistaken for real content made me realize how vulnerable many older adults are to misinformation, deepfakes, and online scams.

At the same time, I also noticed something else: tecnology was not always improving people's lives. I observed how excesive screen time and unbalanced technology use could affect daily life and overall well-being.

These experiences inspired me to create Digital Compass, an educational platform that helps older adults navigate the digital world safety and confidently while also promoting healhier digital habits.
